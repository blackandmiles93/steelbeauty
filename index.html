Restoration and customization

We take the old barn finds, the quiet gems, the moments in time that seem lost to time, and breathe life into them anew.

Restoration

Our restoration division restores 1950's to 1970's classics to their former glory. Sometimes you just can't do everything yourself. We can do total builds from the ground up or partial if you want to make your mark.

Performance

Our performance division can take your existing car and turn into something to remember. Our performance team can turn your car into a full r-spec competitor, or just add some extra horsepower if you're not into that.

Enviro

The future is now. Electric and environmentally friendly cars are no longer the vehicular jokes of the world. Turn your vehicle into a enviro-performance machine and leave everyone in silence biting your zero-emission dust.

Events

Cars are an expression. Show us and the world what car defines you. Showcase and racing events will be hosted in major cities across the US and soon the world.
